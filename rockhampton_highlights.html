Rockhampton Highlights
Private Day Tour

Explore some of the highlights of the Capricorn Region on this all-inclusive tour. Nothing to think about, it's all arranged! Food, drinks, local history, entry to award-winning local attractions, wildlife and scenery. Perfect for visitors who don't have their own transport. Hotel/accommodation pickup and dropoff.

Duration: 1 Day 8:30am - 4:30pm
Cost: From $495 per couple, additional passengers $175 each.
Max: 4 Passengers

Experienced & qualified
tour guide 

Your guide Tony is not only an experienced remote area tour guide, he's also a qualified four wheel drive instructor. He's got a Tourism Degree and a Master's Degree in Wildlife management.  And he's a friendly bloke too!

He'll tell you all about Rockhampton's fascinating history and show you around our area like only a local can.

Highlights Timeline

8:30am

Pick-up

We will pick you up from your accommodation (or breakfast location) anywhere in or near Rockhampton or Yeppoon at 8:30am

Rockhampton History

Learn all about the "Wild West" town that was Rocky in its early days of the gold rush.

Capricorn Caves Tour

A guided tour awaits  you at the multi-award winning Capricaorn Caves, unique because it's an above ground cave.

Coffee and Scones

Morning tea at the Caves after your tour followed by a drive through picturesque farmland.

Koorana Crocodile Farm

After a cooked lunch in the Farm's restaurant, prepare to get up close and personal with these prehistoric beasts. An educational and fun tour of the croc farm.

Mt Archer Scenic Viewpoint

At an elevation of 600m, the views from Mt Archer are spectacular. Take in the vistas over the city and towards the coast.

4:30pm

Drop-off

We'll have you back at your accommodation or a venue of your choice by around 4:30pm.
