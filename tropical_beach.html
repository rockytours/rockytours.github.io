 Adventure Eco-tour FAQs

 What do I need to bring?

Bring your sun hat, walking shoes, swimmers and a towel. Apart from that,  we have everything covered. We provide morning tea, picnic lunch and all the cold drinks you could want. Let us know about any dietery requirements. Also, bring a car seat if you have young children.

What about dangerous animals?

You're lucky to see a snake. No, really! You're lucky because 1) you won't then stand on it, and 2) you hardly ever see one (and they are amazing animals). As for marine stingers (box jellyfish), we don't allow swimming in the sea over the Summer months just in case they are around. If it's a hot day, there is a freshwater stream we can swim in - and no, there's no crocodiles there. Remember, if you see the guide swimming, then it's going to be safe.

Is the tour suitable for young kids?

Absolutely. Kids under 16 are free with 2 paying adults. We can tailor the day to be as easy or strenuous as you like, so catering to kids is easy. Bring your own car seat and we'll strap it in for you. 

How rough is the four wheel driving?

While the sand tracks can be challenging especially when it's dry, the Landcruiser tour vehicle is incredibly capable and comfortable to ride in. Your guide is very experienced in off-road driving and he doesn't drive everywhere as fast as he can, so you don't get bounced to bits!

Where can I get picked up and dropped off?

We will pick you up from your accommodation (or breakfast location) anywhere in or near Rockhampton or Yeppoon at 8:30am and have you back at your accommodation or a venue of your choice by 4:30pm.
