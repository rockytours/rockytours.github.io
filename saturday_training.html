Saturday Courses - Real Off-Road Terrain

Get to know your 4WD Vehicle and Camper Trailer

Saturday courses 8:30am - 4:00pm. 

Specific Off-Road Camper Trailer courses

Maximum 4 vehicles per session - plenty of drive-time

Covers all aspects of 4WDing - hill climbs and descents, water crossing, safe vehicle recoveries and more

Run by a Qualified Instructor (Cert IV TAE (4WD) who is also an experienced tag-along tour leader

Corporate/Promo/Work/Team Building sessions available on request

Private sessions and Ladies Only Days available on request

If you're planning a trip to the Cape, the Outback or the Desert, then our 4WD Driver Training Course will give you the skills and confidence needed to get the most out of your holiday.

Understand exactly what your vehicle (and you) is capable of in a variety of different terrian types and obstacles. 

Great for giving you and your "co-pilot" confidence in where you can take your vehicle.

Also great for younger drivers to learn the safe way to off-road, in particular, the safe way to perform vehicle recoveries with snatch straps and winches.

Our instructor holds a Cert IV in 4WD Training and Assessing. He's also a tag-along tour guide. The experience gained from leading remote tours makes him uniquely qualified to teach others the skills of four wheel driving. Check out more about our instructor here.
